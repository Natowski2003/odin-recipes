Whole Food Eggnog

Description

A healthy way to make a delicious and nutritious holiday dessert

Ingredients (Makes 4 Cups)

  • 1 Cup Heavy Whipping Cream
  • 2 Cups Milk
  • 6 Egg Yolks
  • Maple Syrup or Honey to Taste
  • 1/4 Tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 1/2 Tsp Nutmeg
  • Ceylon Cinnamon
  • Pinch of Sea Salt

Steps

  1. Whisk egg yolks and syrup until homogenous and creamy.
  2. In a saucepan over medium-high heat, mix cream, milk, nutmeg, and salt until it reaches a simmer.
  3. Slowly add milk mixture to egg mixture while whisking intensely.
  4. After the two have combined, pour the mixture back into the saucepan.
  5. Whisk constantly until mixture has slightly thickened.
  6. Pour through a fine strainer into container of choice and cover the surface with wrap to avoid a skin.
  7. Refrigerate until completely cooled.
  8. Serve with a sprinkle of cinnamon and/or nutmeg if desired.
  9. Can be refriedgerated for up to one week.
